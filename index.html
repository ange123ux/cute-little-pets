Our story

Cute Little pets Ltd is a company that started operating since 2015, and it was invented by Ange Uwimana, the Marketing Coordinator and Web Developer of Zobra company. Cute Little Pets Ltd has a mission of providing a home to pets for the people who may need to go on a vacation but who don't plan to go with them. We take care of everything that a pet needs. Before leaving your pet to us, we require you to pay $300 and it is not refundable. This money is for looking after your pet in times of emergency such as diseases, insurances,... We can't wait to see you!!!
